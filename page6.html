APPROVED BY QA TEAM

Always make sure that the Card has a QC Passed Label

ARTWORK REVIEW RESULT

Artwork Review is a feature that allows customers to view their artwork prior to printing. If there is ORDERED ART REVIEW Label in the card we need to check additional Label before ordering.

Here are the labels:

1. NO RESPONSE TO AWR - Customer failed to respond within 24 Hours.

2. ARTWORK APPROVED - Customer Approves the artwork.

if you see one of the two labels above we can proceed.

In the event that customer asks for revision, you will see the request in the comment section of the card. In this case once the Revision has been made just look for QC Passed Label and we can proceed.
