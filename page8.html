Frequently Asked Questions

General
Q. Do we need to check every address for every order if it's valid or not?
A. If we have few orders, then we should check every single one of them since we have time, but if there are a lot of orders for example during peak/holiday season, then you will have to decide for yourself which one to check base on your observation and instinct if it's valid or not most orders with View Map indicated in Shopify have a valid address.
Q. As I check Shopify, the item that needs to be ordered was labeled as "REMOVED" or "REFUNDED", what should I do?
If there's only 1 item in that order, transfer this card to "ORDER ISSUE" list then add a comment stating, that this item was removed/refunded in shopify. But if there are multiple items that have a label of "UNFULFILLED" then process those items excluding the one that have a removed/refunded label.
Q. As I was ordering this card, I noticed that I already ordered it before, what should I do?
A. We always advised all agents to check if there are duplicate cards before starting, in this case, copy the order # then search it in trello, if it shows 2 or more cards that have the same order #, then transfer this card to "issue" list and comment something like, "this card is duplicate" or just a simple "duplicate"
 Q: What if the the card does not have a "DIGITAL ART FILE" label but has a "Digital Art File of Your Pet" on its description?
A: Go to shopify and check the order list if it has a "Digital Art File of Your Pet" order. If it has a DAF order, transfer the card to "ORDERS WITH DAF" list after placing the order
Q: I accidentally Archived a card from my list, what should I do?
A: Search for the card number and click the button "Send to board" from the Actions menu on the lower right part of the card
Q: What if there are revision notes included in the comments section of the card that has not been addressed?
A: Transfer this card to "ISSUE" list then add a comment stating that the artwork has not been revised

Q. As per cx notes, they want their pet to have a some sort of accessory, but there's nothing in the artwork, what should I do?
A. Technically we don't add accessory, tho if the original photo also include an accessory then we just proceed with what the artist gave us as long as there's a QC PASSED label attached to that card.
Q. There are multiple pets artwork attached in the card, which one should I use?
A. First go to shopify and check the image link of that specific order to see which of those pets should be on that item, next check the comment section in the card and look for the CSR comments, there will be a comment that states how many more pet should be on that item and their original photo. Lastly just to make sure, check the customers note, they will probably say that, they wanted to add more pets in that item but won't able to send more than 1 photo because of the limit. If you are not sure please ask your TLs.
